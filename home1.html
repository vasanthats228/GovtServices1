Frequently Asked Questions

Register using your Aadhaar number or mobile OTP on the portal. Complete your profile with basic details to access all services. You can also use DigiLocker for automatic profile setup.

Requirements vary by service. Generally, you need ID proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID), address proof, and service-specific documents. Most documents can be fetched automatically from DigiLocker.

Most services have nominal government fees as applicable. Payment can be made online through net banking, UPI, credit/debit cards. All payments are secure and encrypted.

Login to your dashboard and view real-time status of all your applications with unique tracking ID. You'll also receive SMS and email updates at every stage.
