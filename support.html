Common Issues & Solutions

Quick fixes for frequently encountered problems with government e-services portals.

Aadhaar OTP not received?

Check if mobile number is registered with Aadhaar. Wait for 3 minutes and request again. Max 5 attempts per day.

Passport appointment not available?

New slots open at 10:00 AM daily. Check nearby Passport Seva Kendras. Tatkal scheme available for urgent travel.

Driving License test booking failed?

Clear browser cache, use Chrome/Edge. Ensure all documents uploaded are below 2MB. Try after 24 hours.

PAN card payment failed?

Money will be refunded within 5-7 working days. Try again with different payment method. Check transaction status.
