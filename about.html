Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register for services?
Register using your Aadhaar number or mobile OTP on our portal. Complete your profile in 2 minutes.
What documents are needed?
Requirements vary by service. Generally, you need ID proof (Aadhaar/Voter ID) and address proof.
Is there any service fee?
Most services have nominal government fees as applicable. Payment can be made online securely.
How to track my application?
Login to your dashboard and view real-time status of all your applications with tracking ID.
